  • Q

    TYPES OF PROOF NEEDED WHEN APPLYING FOR FESTIVAL BADGES AND CINEPHILE BADGES

    A

    When applying for a Festival badge, attach one of the following documents to prove that you work in the film industry.
    - If you work for a film-related company or organization, a certificate of employment issued within the last three months (Business card or employee ID is not acceptable)
    ※ If you are in a new company or organization, which has not been registered for BIFF, please submit a company profile and a list of recent film-related projects via email.
    - Contents that can prove your filmography and movie production/promotion/distribution/investment history
    ※ IMDB link including personal identification information (date of birth)
    ※ Captured images of the ending credit with information of the film and your participation.

    When applying for the Cinephile Badge, attach the following documents to prove you are studying film or video.
    - Proof of enrollment or leave of absence dated within the last one month (student ID is not acceptable).

  • Q

    PHOTO GUIDELINE

    A

    For identification purposes, your badge photo must meet the following requirements
    - A clear, high-quality color photo with your face facing forward
    - No photos with hats, headphones, sunglasses, etc.
    - Photos taken within the last 6 months
    - File format is JPG only
    - Business card size of 354*472 (3*4 ratio)
    - File name must be in Korean or English without any special characters
    * If an error occurs when uploading or saving a photo, please try renaming the file or try with a new photo.

  • Q

    WHEN CAN I GET AN APPROVAL MAIL? / DELETED THE MAIL

    A

    Once we receive your accreditation, we'll review your documents and decide whether to approve.
    You will be notified within 3 days (excluding weekends and holidays).
    Sometimes official emails are categorized as spam, so please check your spam folder before contacting us.

    If you received a rejection email, it means you don't have the necessary qualifications to get the badge. In this case, you won't receive a payment email.

    If your email for registration or payment has been deleted, please contact us by email.

  • Q

    PAYMENT PROCESS / GROUP PAYMENT

    A

    After the accreditation is approved, please make the payment on the page emailed to you. (Payment is available with credit/debit card. Cash is unavailable)

    ※ For Festival or Cinephile badges, group payment is available for organizations of 20 people or more. If you wish to make a group payment, please contact us in advance.

  • Q

    CANCELLATION

    A

    You can get a refund until 15:00 on Friday, September 20 for Festival badge and 15:00 on Wednesday, September 11 for Cinephile badge. You will get a refund after deducting 30% of the fee.
    (Cancellations can be made without any fee if a refund request is submitted within 12 hours from the time of payment.)
    After the deadline, refunds are not possible. Refunds may take 7-10 business days to complete, depending on your credit card company.

    Refund Policy for Press Badge
    - There will be a registration fee of 50,000 KRW.
    - Note that you can receive a refund of a 30% deduction of the original fee due to our policy.

  • Q

    BADGE LOST

    A

    Please wear your badge at all times, as you will not be able to access your badge benefits without it.
    When you enter the theater using the film ticket issued by your badge, we will check your badge with your ticket.
    When reissuing a badge due to loss or damage, there is a KRW 10,000 reissue fee for the Festival/Cinephile/Press/Platform Busan badge and a KRW 20,000 for the Market badge.
    Once the badge is reissued, the previous badge will become invalid.

  • Q

    ON-SITE BADGE ISSUANCE

    A

    Festival badge on-site issuance is only available for those who have the following documents.
    - If you are in a new company or organization, which has not been registered for BIFF, please submit your company's business license and company introduction.
    - If you are applying under an already registered company, please submit at least one of the following: employment certificate (issued within the last 3 months), personal biography, article, or IMDB link. (Business card or employee ID is not acceptable.)
    - If you are a freelancer, please submit at least one of the following: a personal portrait, an internet article, or a captured image of the ending credit with your name on it.
    - If you are a film major professor, please submit your tenure certificate; if you are a non-film major professor, please submit a syllabus showing your film and video courses.

    For the Press badge accreditation, please fill the on-site registration form and pay an administrative fee of KRW 50,000.
