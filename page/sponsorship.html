Sponsorship

The Busan International Film Festival is the best film festival in Asia that presents Korean and Asian films to the global film industry. With differentiated programs and films that reflect diverse perspectives of Asian directors, the Busan International Film Festival provides a novel experience to the audience. To contribute to the development of Asian film industry, the Busan International Film Festival operates various programs for support such as fostering talents, production support, and distribution.
The Busan International Film Festival provides various marketing opportunities through its sponsorship program. It provides brand exposure, advertisements, invitation to the film festival, and other benefits to the companies that participate in the sponsorship, and new collaborative opportunities can be developed according to the sponsor's needs.
