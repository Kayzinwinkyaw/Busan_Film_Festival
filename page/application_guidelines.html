Application Guidelines

You can scroll left and right to view the content.
Timeline
  • Online Application Deadline: April 12, 2024 (18:00 KST)
  • Final Selection Announcement: End of June
  • Submission of Short Film Script: July 7, 2024 (18:00 KST)
  • Online Pre-production: July 15 - September 21, 2024
  • Program Dates: September 22-October 11, 2024
Eligibility
  • Who has Asian nationality, and overseas Korean (Age 18 or older) seeking one's dream and future in cinema
    • * Regarding nationality, eligibility, and definition of overseas Korean, please refer to our FAQ
  • Who has experience in directing, producing, cinematography, editing, production design or sound at least two short films
  • Who can communicate in English
  • Who submits all required documents and supporting materials on time
  • Who can attend all sessions including online pre-production
  • Who has never attended Asian Film Academy / CHANEL X BIFF Asian Film Academy before
Application Materials
  • All documents should be in English
  • Application Form (Application will be accepted until April 12 via online application form only)
  • Personal Statement
    • - Introduction of yourself in relation to film work
    • - The reason why you wish to attend the program
    • - Your future plans in film work
  • A Letter of Recommendation with the recommender's contact information & signature * Optional
  • Portfolio in directing, producing, cinematography, or other major role (downloadable file(s))
    • * Must have English subtitle, if its dialogue is not in English
    • * Accepted video film formats : AVI, FLV, MOV, MP4, MPEG-2, or WMV
    • * If you choose to upload a MOV file, make sure that it is not using the Apple ProRes compression.
         We do not support reading Apple ProRes files or raw camera formats like ARRI and RED at this time.
    • * In case of a film over 40 min in length, please make it a 15 min-clip for submission
    • * Total running time required is under 40 minutes
How to Apply
  • Once you fill out your online application form
  • Upload requirements below on the form
    • * Scanned letter of recommendation in PDF / JPG format (Optional)
    • * Web-link for your portfolio-work
    • * The link must be downloadable, not streaming link.
  • After clicking the ‘Submit’ button at the end of the application form, the applicant will receive an Edit Link via e-mail. The Edit Link allows the applicant to modify the application form up until the final deadline.
Additional Information
  • The cost for travelling within Asia, accommodation, meals, and equipment will be provided to all selected fellows.
  • English interview will be conducted via Zoom for those who pass the initial screening.
