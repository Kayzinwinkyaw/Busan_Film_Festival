Platform BUSAN

  • Platform BUSAN

Joint Growth Network for Asian Independent Filmmakers!

Platform BUSAN aims to welcome new and emerging Asian filmmakers to Busan providing an opportunity of global networking.

As an annual networking event organized by Busan International Film Festival (BIFF) and Asian Contents & Film Market (ACFM), Platform BUSAN hosts various live events such as master classes, seminars and showcases to enlighten artistic philosophies and to exchange field experiences.

The detailed program schedule will be announced in September. Please keep in mind all Platform BUSAN programs are conducted in English only.
