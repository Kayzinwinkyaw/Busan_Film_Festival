Press

Press badges are granted to journalists affiliated with official media platforms (print media, radio, television, online press, press agency, and magazines) to assist in their coverage of the festival and market.

Press Badge Type

Press

For correspondents affiliated with official media platforms (print media, radio, television, online press, press agencies, and magazines)

  • Correspondents and editors for print, online media or news agencies
  • Correspondent producers, writers, program planners, and TV and radio reporters
  • Critics & contributors who regularly contribute film critiques and relevant articles to the media (limited to those who have been active within the past year)

※ Press badge holders are not eligible to apply for the Opening/Closing Ceremony pass

Press T (Technician)

For media employees or outsourced filming agencies responsible for the technical aspects of coverage, including photography and camera operation

  • e.g. Photographers, Camera operators/assistants, Sound/Audio technicians, Lighting, and Editing staff

※ Press T (Technician) badge holders are eligible to apply for Opening/Closing Ceremony passes.

Press Badge Application Period

  • General August 1 (Thu) - 18:00 (KST) September 13 (Fri) FREE
  • On-site October 1 (Tue) - October 11 (Fri) Administrative Fee KRW 50,000

※ Please make sure to check the registration deadline.

How to Apply

Register online within the application period (*attach required documents) → Review application (within 4 business days from the day of registration) → Notification of approval status (Approved or denied)
  • Required Documents
    • 1. A copy of business card
    • 2. Required document(choose one)

      1) Link to a sample article (written within the past year)

      2) A screenshot image or URL of program credits (within the past year)

    • 3. (New Attendees Only) Recommendation letter with a signature from the editor or the desk ↓ Download Form

Press Badge Benefits

  • Up to 4 complimentary tickets for different screenings a day
  • Exclusive Access to Ticket Desk for Press
  • Online Ticket Reservation for Press
  • Access to Festival/Market Venues
  • P&I (Press & Industry) Screening available
  • Access to Press Screening
  • Access to Press Center
  • Video Library & Preview Room Available
  • Access to Guest Visit(GV)
  • Press Package

※ Separate passes are required for Opening/Closing Photo Zone

※ Press Badge does NOT grant access to Opening/Closing ceremonies.
