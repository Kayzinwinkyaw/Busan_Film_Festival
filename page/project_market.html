Start of the Most Promising Asian Film Projects!

Launched in 1998 at the 3rd Busan International Film Festival, Asian Project Market (APM, formerly Pusan Promotion Plan; PPP) has connected talented Asian directors with booming new projects to professionals from film industry world-wide.

Ranging from big-scale commercial film projects to low-budget indie ones, each year APM brings together fresh feature film projects and global film investors, producers, and distributors. Numerous APM projects have already made it through to completion and received positive responses from both film festivals and international audiences. As such, APM continues to earn greater interest from filmmakers around the world.

The 27th Asian Project Market will take place for four days from 5th to 8th October in 2024. Please feel free to contact APM team for any inquiries about participation in APM as either project teams or industry guests.
