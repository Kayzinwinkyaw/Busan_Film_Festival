Film education program for promising filmmakers who will lead Asian cinema

CHANEL X BIFF Asian Film Academy will select 24 promising filmmakers from different Asian countries to participate in the program that includes short film production, workshops, mentoring and masterclasses. The completed short films by the fellows will be officially screened at the Busan International Film Festival. The program will receive applications from emerging talents in cinema from March 4 (Mon) to April 12 (Fri).
